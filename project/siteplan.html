Overview

Site Name

MSE - Movie Store Engine

Purpose

The purpose of this movie-based website is to provide users with a platform to search and access information about various movies. It aims to be an informative and engaging resource for movie enthusiasts of all ages, offering detailed information and updates on popular and trending movies, as well as the option to store and manage custom movie lists.

Scenarios

  • What are the trending movies currently?
  • What are the most popular movies today?
  • How can I get specific information about a movie?
